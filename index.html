Introducing Smart Patient

We serve u with our best

Smart patient is system that analyzes the patients report and provides the recommendations to be healthy and careful about their health. As well as it helps to make the reporting and storing of digital data easy in hospitals. It is a patient care and monitor system for hospitals and people proposed as final year project. It is the application package that helps a patient of a hospital to operate smartly. The system is proposed to facilitate users about time and money saving, easy accessible of service, manage reports and files in public area like hospitals providing useable services and care for their health. It aims to cure patients easily and quickly after the treatments from hospital and get the service in easy manner in hospitals and homes.


WHY Smart Patient?

We design your dream

This system is user independent. Both register and unregistered user can use the system for their services and get advantage from it. Registered user have certain extended feature but normal visitor also user it normally. Explanation on how the recommendation system works can be provided by explicitly listing content feature or description that caused an item to occur in the list of recommendations. Those feature are indicators to consult in order to decide whether to trust a recommendation. Since it is the web application anyone can access it from anywhere any get their stored content easily. It can be used by both hospital and patients. Hospital can use it to manage their report records and patients to check their health status and get suggestions to be healthy.
